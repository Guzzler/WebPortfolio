## Implementation Notes
- Replace the current `Technical` tool dump with two or three curated "Studio Notes" cards or modules instead of long undifferentiated tag clouds.
- Anchor the cards in grounded portfolio facts that are already present:
  - Luma AI and Persona for ambitious AI product systems
  - AISOC, OpenShiksha, and VaccinePost for public-interest and open-source work
  - Unity games, sports, and board games for play, competition, and interaction feel
- Keep skill detail visible, but demote it into compact supporting ribbons, short grouped stacks, or sub-notes rather than making the tool list the main event.
- Rework `In My Down Time` so it feels like part of the same story instead of a separate lifestyle box. The section should show how off-hours play sharpens product instincts rather than reading as filler.
- Use light compositional cues, not heavy chrome: one memorable visual idea, clean spacing, and a short bridge into the Games section if needed.

## Responsive And Accessibility Goals
- The new right-column shelf should collapse into a clean single-column stack on tablet and mobile without horizontal overflow or dense chip wrapping.
- Any new cards, chips, or links should keep visible keyboard focus states in both dark and light mode.
- Preserve readable paragraph lengths and avoid decorative treatments that reduce contrast or make the right rail feel heavy.
- Make sure the revised structure still scans logically for screen-reader users with clear section headings and compact supporting text.
